---
title: Non-Modifiable texture slots for shaders
tags: [Unity, Shaders]
type: tip
created: 2021-12-28T01:24:00+04:00
source: Silent
---

You can use a [NonModifableTextureData] attribute on texture properties in shaders to make them “read-only”. Allowing you to distribute pre-baked texture user can’t mess with.

You can then set the texture value on the Shader Importer directly.
