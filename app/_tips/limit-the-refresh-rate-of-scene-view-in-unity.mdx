---
title: Limit the refresh rate of Scene View in Unity
tags: [Unity]
type: tip
created: 2024-09-16T13:36:30.583Z
---
You can limit the refresh rate of the scene view by setting Preferences -> General -> Interaction Mode to "Monitor Refresh Rate".
